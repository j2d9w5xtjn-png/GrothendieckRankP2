## 2. Tangent-square lemma

Write

\[
 [2]^\#=e+\Psi_1+\text{terms of filtration at least two}.
\]

After exact row reduction of the linearized bialgebra equations, the matrix
of `Psi_1` on the augmentation basis `(e_1,e_2,e_3)` has one of the following
forms.  Rows are outputs and columns are inputs:

\[
\begin{array}{c|c}

\begin{pmatrix}0&0&0\\0&0&0\\a&b&0\end{pmatrix},

\]

The same four normal forms occur in the equal and mixed charts.  In
particular,

\[
 \boxed{\Psi_1^2=0.}
\]

Since the quadratic initial form of `[4]^#-e` is `Psi_1^2`, all nine
quadratic targets vanish modulo the ideal generated by the **linear**
bialgebra equations.  No quadratic bialgebra relation is needed for this
vanishing.  Equivalently, each target has a degree-two certificate of the
form

\[
 \operatorname{in}_2(T)+\sum_p h_p\ell_p=0,
\]

where the `ell_p` are RREF combinations of the original linear equations and
the `h_p` are linear forms.  The script can print the exact bit-packed
selectors with `--certificates`.

This is a useful hand lemma: every first-order bialgebra deformation of any
of the four geometric local--local fibers has square-zero first-order
doubling symbol.

## 3. Complete degree-three construction

For auditability, here is the filtered algorithm.  Suppose
`f_i=ell_i+(higher)` are the integral equations.

1. Row-reduce the `ell_i`, retaining exact equation selectors.  Lift a basis
   of the linear row space to actual equations `G_p`, and lift a basis of all
   constant linear relations to exact sums `H_c`.
2. The elements

   \[
    x_jG_p,\quad 2G_p,\quad H_c
   \]

   span `(I intersect q^2)/(I intersect q^3)`.  Row-reduce their exact
   degree-two initials, again retaining relation selectors.
3. Degree three is spanned by all forty-six parameter multiples of a basis
   of the degree-two row space, together with the exact degree-three carries
   obtained from every constant relation among the degree-two spanning
   elements.

This is exhaustive: coefficients of an arbitrary ideal combination are
expanded successively modulo `q`, `q^2`, and `q^3`.  At each level, the
constant residue is precisely one of the computed kernel relations and the
positive-order part is a parameter multiple of the preceding initial row
space.

As an independent check in equal characteristic, the script also constructs
all 175,122 products

\[
 x^\nu f_i,\qquad |\nu|\leq2,
\]

in the full 17,295-dimensional jet algebra `F2[x]/m^4`.  Its direct ranks
equal

\[
 \operatorname{rank}\operatorname{gr}_1(I)+
 \operatorname{rank}\operatorname{gr}_2(I)+
 \operatorname{rank}\operatorname{gr}_3(I)
\]

on every chart, providing an independent check of the filtered recursion.
